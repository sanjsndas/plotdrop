Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?
You can schedule a free, no-obligation consultation by filling out the form above, calling us at (555) 289-7700, or emailing hello@plotdrop.com. We'll work with you to find a convenient time.

What areas do you serve?
We serve the greater Portland metropolitan area, including Beaverton, Hillsboro, Gresham, Vancouver, and surrounding communities. If you're outside our standard service area, please contact us—we may still be able to accommodate your project.

Do you offer free estimates?
Yes! We provide free, detailed estimates for all projects. Our estimates include material costs, labor, and a projected timeline. We'll visit your property, assess your needs, and provide a comprehensive quote within 48 hours.

What types of edging materials do you work with?
We specialize in steel, natural stone, wood, brick, concrete, and composite edging. We also offer custom solutions for unique projects. During your consultation, we'll help you choose the best material for your landscape, budget, and aesthetic preferences.

Do you offer warranties on your work?
Yes, we stand behind our work with a 12-month workmanship warranty. Materials are covered by their respective manufacturer warranties. Our team is committed to quality and will address any issues that arise during the warranty period.

How long does a typical project take?
Most residential projects are completed within 2–5 days, depending on the scope and complexity. Larger commercial projects may take longer. We provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase and keep you updated throughout the process.

Can I see examples of your previous work?
Absolutely! We have a portfolio of completed projects available on our website and in our showroom. We can also provide references from satisfied clients upon request.

Do you handle permits and approvals?
We can assist with obtaining necessary permits and approvals, though this may incur additional fees. We recommend discussing this during your consultation so we can plan accordingly.

Still have questions? Our friendly team is always ready to help. Call us at (555) 289-7700 or email hello@plotdrop.com and we'll be happy to assist you.

About Plot Drop

Plot Drop Lawn Edging has been serving the Portland community since 2012. We specialize in creating beautiful, durable, and functional lawn edges that enhance the value and appeal of your property. Our team of experienced landscapers and designers is passionate about delivering exceptional results that exceed expectations.

We take pride in our attention to detail, use of high-quality materials, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you're a homeowner looking to improve your garden's curb appeal or a commercial property manager seeking professional landscaping solutions, Plot Drop is your trusted partner.

Our mission is to transform ordinary lawns into stunning landscapes through precision edging, expert craftsmanship, and personalized service. We believe that every property deserves a beautiful frame, and we're dedicated to making that vision a reality.

We're also committed to sustainability and use eco-friendly practices whenever possible. We recycle materials, minimize waste, and offer sustainable edging options that are both beautiful and environmentally responsible.

Thank you for considering Plot Drop for your lawn edging needs. We look forward to working with you and helping you create the outdoor space you've always wanted.
