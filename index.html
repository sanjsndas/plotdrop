Why Plot Drop?

since 2012 Plot Drop has been the go-to lawn edging specialist for homeowners, landscapers, and property managers. We don’t just cut grass — we sculpt the boundary between your lawn and garden, driveway, or patio. Our edging services transform ordinary yards into magazine-worthy landscapes.

With over 4,200 completed projects, we understand that edging is both an art and a science. From deep cuts to graceful curves, every edge we install is designed to last, reduce maintenance, and enhance the natural beauty of your property. We use premium materials: powder-coated steel, kiln-dried hardwood, natural stone, and high-strength concrete.

Our edging services

  • Steel edging – durable, rust-resistant, and perfect for modern landscapes. Available in black, bronze, and galvanized finishes.
  • Natural stone – granite, limestone, and flagstone for timeless elegance. We hand-select each stone for color and texture.
  • Wood & timber – pressure-treated pine, cedar, and redwood. Ideal for cottage gardens and rustic settings.
  • Brick & concrete – classic and versatile. We offer herringbone, basket weave, and soldier course patterns.
  • Composite & recycled – eco-conscious choices that mimic wood or stone without the maintenance.

We also install integrated lighting and drip irrigation along edges, making your landscape functional and beautiful day and night. Our team is trained in proper drainage techniques to prevent water pooling and soil erosion.

Custom design & consultation

Every property is unique, and we treat it that way. Our design team works with you to create a custom edging plan that complements your home’s architecture and garden style. We use advanced CAD software to simulate the finished look, so you can visualize the outcome before we break ground.

We’ll help you choose the right height, width, and depth for your edging. Factors like soil type, climate, and foot traffic are all considered. We also advise on companion plants that thrive alongside edging features — such as lavender, boxwood, or ornamental grasses.

Our consultation is always free, and we provide a detailed quote with transparent pricing. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Installation process

  1. Site preparation – We mark the edging line, remove turf, and prepare the base with proper compaction.
  2. Material installation – Whether it’s steel, stone, or wood, we install with precision using professional-grade tools.
  3. Finishing touches – We backfill soil, add mulch or gravel, and ensure the edge is perfectly level.
  4. Cleanup & review – We leave your property spotless and walk you through the finished work.

Most residential projects are completed within 2–5 days, depending on the complexity. We work efficiently without sacrificing quality.

Maintenance & longevity

Properly installed edging can last for decades. Steel edges require occasional rust treatment (we offer a re-coating service), while stone and concrete are virtually maintenance-free. Wood edges benefit from annual sealing to prevent rot.

We provide a free maintenance check after 12 months, and our warranty covers any manufacturing defects or installation issues. Our team is always available for touch-ups, adjustments, or expansions.

Plot Drop also offers seasonal edge sharpening and redefinition services to keep your landscape looking crisp year-round.
